The 1974 Lamborghini Countach LP400 is the inaugural, purest production version of the iconic supercar, produced between 1974 and 1978.

It features a 3.9 liter naturally aspirated v12 engine producig 370 - 375 hp at 8000 rpm and 266lb of torque, with a lightweight, narrow body, rear wheel drive, and a 5 speed manual gearbox.
